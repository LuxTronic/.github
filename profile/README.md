## üè≠ What We Build

LuxTronic designs **real-time, high-throughput inspection systems** for high-volume manufacturing:

- **Aluminium Cans:** dents/dings, rim/neck deformation, flange damage, varnish/print defects, internal coating, ends & tabs (score, rivet, curl)  
- **Glass Bottles:** finish/neck cracks, base checks, inclusions, sidewall cracks, contamination, blister/cord/stones  
- **360¬∞ Vision Rigs:** multi-camera area/line-scan, strobed lighting, sub-ms triggering, deterministic reject timing  
- **Edge AI Systems:** sub-millisecond inference across Jetson/x86; optimized pipelines with deterministic latency  
- **Industrial Integration:** encoders, digital I/O, rejecters; OPC UA / MQTT / Modbus; PLC, SCADA, MES connectivity  

Our systems produce **rich defect tracking and per-unit lineage** (time, lane, turret/mold/cavity) and feed into SPC dashboards, trend analytics, and automated response logic.
